## RADIUS Authentication Removed

RADIUS

PostgreSQL 18 and below supported the RADIUS authentication protocol. Information about an alternative way to configure RADIUS support is available on the PostgreSQL [wiki](https://wiki.postgresql.org/wiki/RADIUS).
