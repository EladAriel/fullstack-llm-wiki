# Downgrade |newversion| to |oldversion|

.. include:: /includes/7.0-upgrade-replacements.rst

> **Important:**  Starting in MongoDB 7.0, binary downgrades are no longer supported for
 MongoDB Community Edition.

Starting in MongoDB 7.0, you cannot downgrade your Enterprise deployment's binary version without assistance from support.

To downgrade from |newversion| to |oldversion|, [submit a support request](https://support.mongodb.com/) or contact your technical support representative.
