Starting in MongoDB 5.1, the :pipeline:`$lookup` and :pipeline:`$graphLookup` aggregation stages support `sharded collections <sharded-cluster>` in the `from` parameter.

In previous versions of MongoDB, `$lookup` and `$graphLookup` only allowed for unsharded `from` collections.
