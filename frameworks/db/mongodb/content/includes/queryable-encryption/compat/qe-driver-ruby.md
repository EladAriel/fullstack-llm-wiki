### Driver Compatibility

To use {+qe+} with the :driver:`Ruby </ruby>` driver, install driver version 2.19 or later and [libmongocrypt-helper](https://rubygems.org/gems/libmongocrypt-helper/)_ version {+minimum-libmongocrypt-version+} or later.
