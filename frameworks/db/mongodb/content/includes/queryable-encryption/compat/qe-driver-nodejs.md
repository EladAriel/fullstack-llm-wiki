### Driver Compatibility

To use {+qe+} with the :driver:`Node.js </node/current>` driver, install driver version 5.5.0 or later and [mongodb-client-encryption](https://www.npmjs.com/package/mongodb-client-encryption/)_ version 2.8.0 or later.
