> **Warning:** Before you bind your instance to a publicly-accessible IP address,
you must secure your cluster from unauthorized access. For a complete
list of security recommendations, see
`security-checklist`. At minimum, consider
`enabling authentication <checklist-auth>` and :ref:`hardening
network infrastructure <network-config-hardening>`.
