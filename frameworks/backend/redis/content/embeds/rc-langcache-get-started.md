To set up LangCache on Redis Cloud:

1. [Create a database]({{< relref "/operate/rc/databases/create-database" >}}) on Redis Cloud.
2. [Create a LangCache service]({{< relref "/operate/rc/context-engine/langcache/create-service" >}}) for your database on Redis Cloud.
3. [Use the LangCache API]({{< relref "/operate/rc/context-engine/langcache/use-langcache" >}}) from your client app.

After you set up LangCache, you can [view and edit the cache]({{< relref "/operate/rc/context-engine/langcache/view-edit-cache" >}}) and [monitor the cache's performance]({{< relref "/operate/rc/context-engine/langcache/monitor-cache" >}}).

See also our [Redis LangCache setup](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UOGhMZlZLko)
tutorial video for advice on how to get started.
